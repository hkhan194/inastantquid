All you have to so is fill out the FORM and we will give you a call in your prefered time. It only takes 2 minutes to fill out the form from our side with your basic information.

Yes! you will be accepted with whatever credit history you have. Here at InstantQuid will find a loan which is most suitable for you. Instant Lending gives 100% guareented loan approval.

Can I reborrow even if I already have a loan with Instant Lending?

You can reborrow from us for the second time all you have to do is follow the same process for applying fill out the APPLY form and we will look up your details and current loan repayments and make a desicion.

What is the amount I can borrow from Instant Lending?

You can borrow from £100 to £10,000. Each loan is for a fixed term and different reapyment options depending your circumstances. But our flexible overpayment plans makes it easy to repay early. If you would like to get an idea of how much you will repay (and save) if you repay early request a callback and we will answer your questions.

When will the money be in my bank account?

Once approved, we will send your money within 10 minutes. If your bank accepts Faster Payments, then you may have access to the money straight away, but it depends on how quickly your bank processes deposits.
